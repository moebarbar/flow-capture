Add full integrations and automation capabilities to the platform, focusing on team collaboration, task management, HR triggers, content platforms, analytics, and billing. The integrations should include:

1. Team Communication & Collaboration:

Slack: send guide notifications, step reminders, and comments directly in channels.

Microsoft Teams: push guide updates, assignments, and workflow notifications.

Email (SendGrid or similar): send notifications for sign-ups, guide assignments, approvals, and workflow updates.

2. Project & Task Management:

Jira: assign guide steps as tasks, track progress, link guide completion to Jira workflows.

ClickUp & Monday.com: sync tasks and guide steps with team boards.

Optional: Asana or Trello for task tracking.

3. HR & Onboarding Systems:

BambooHR, Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, or Gusto: automatically assign onboarding guides when a new employee is added.

Google Workspace / Microsoft 365: auto-assign guides and manage user access.

4. Content & Knowledge Platforms:

Notion, Confluence, SharePoint: push finalized guides or SOPs into company knowledge bases.

Google Drive / Dropbox: sync guides for storage or offline access.

5. Automation & Workflow Tools:

Zapier / Make (Integromat): trigger automated workflows based on guide actions (e.g., new user added → assign guide; guide step completed → notify manager).

Webhooks: allow push updates or alerts to any custom system.

6. Analytics & Reporting:

Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude: track guide usage, step completion, and workflow efficiency.

Optional BI tools (Tableau, PowerBI) for exporting dashboards.

7. Payment & Billing:

Stripe: handle subscriptions, team upgrades, and per-user billing.

Optional: PayPal / Paddle for international payment options.

Requirements:

All integrations should be secure, role-based, and configurable from the admin dashboard.

Automation triggers should be scalable and allow multiple actions per event.

Ensure smooth interaction with web guides, dashboards, and AI-driven features.

The goal is to make the platform highly connected, automated, and enterprise-ready, enabling users to push guides, track progress, and automate workflows seamlessly.
